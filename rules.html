Memory Game Rules

Welcome to the Memory Game! Here are the rules:

  1. The game consists of a set of cards with matching pairs.
  2. Shuffle the cards and place them face down on the playing area.
  3. Players take turns flipping two cards at a time to find matching pairs.
  4. If a player finds a matching pair, they get a point and continue their turn.
  5. If the cards do not match, they are flipped back, and it's the next player's turn.
